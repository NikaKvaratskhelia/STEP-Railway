Frequently Asked Questions

Tickets

How do I download my train ticket?
You can download your ticket from the email confirmation or your account's ticket history.
Can I cancel a ticket after purchase?
Yes, cancellations are possible up to 24 hours before departure via your account dashboard.
I didn't receive a confirmation email. What should I do?
Please check your spam folder, or contact our support with your booking ID.

Accounts

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password" to receive a reset link.
How can I update my personal information?
You can update your profile details in your account settings.
Why is my account locked?
Accounts are locked after multiple failed login attempts. Contact support to unlock it.

Payments

Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, PayPal, and selected mobile payments.
I was charged twice. How do I get a refund?
Contact support with your order ID and both charge references for quick assistance.
Is online payment secure on your website?
Yes, we use encrypted payment gateways to ensure your data is safe.
